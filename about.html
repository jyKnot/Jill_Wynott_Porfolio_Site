Everyone has a story. Here's mine.

I didn’t fall into this field - I pursued it with purpose. Design, for me, isn’t just about making things look good; it’s about making things work for people, for communities, and for the goals that matter.

I’ve built a career around impact. Every project I take on starts with a problem worth solving, and a story worth telling.

Behind the pixels, I carry the same grit and curiosity into my everyday life. I’ve earned my B license in skydiving, sailed the Bahamas on a 27-foot boat and skied from the summit of Mount Baker. That same spirit of exploration and determination shows up in how I design, how I collaborate, and how I solve problems.

I built this portfolio to show what I’ve done, and more importantly, how I think.



Thanks for being here.
